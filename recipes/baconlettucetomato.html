Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Cook bacon in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  2. Drain bacon on a paper towel-lined plate.
  3. Arrange cooked bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices on one slice of bread.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on the other slice of bread.
  5. Close to make a sandwich.
